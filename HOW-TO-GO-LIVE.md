## STEP 1 — Create a FREE Netlify account
1. Go to: https://netlify.com
2. Click "Sign Up" — use your email or Google account
3. It's completely FREE — no credit card needed

---

## STEP 2 — Upload your files
1. After signing in, you'll see a big area that says:
   "Drag and drop your site folder here"
2. Create a folder on your computer called: archiedraft-ai
3. Put ALL 3 files inside that folder
4. Drag the entire folder onto the Netlify page
5. Wait about 30 seconds...

---

## STEP 3 — Your site is LIVE! 🎉
Netlify will give you a FREE link like:
   https://amazing-architect-123.netlify.app

Share this link with ANYONE in the world!

---

## STEP 4 — Get a custom domain (optional)
To have www.archiedraft.ai instead of the random link:
1. Go to: https://namecheap.com
2. Search "archiedraft.ai" — costs about $15/year
3. In Netlify, go to: Site Settings → Domain Management → Add domain
4. Follow the instructions to connect it

---

## IMPORTANT — About the AI features:
The AI chat and conflict detection use the Anthropic API.
When you share the app publicly, you will need your own API key.

To get one:
1. Go to: https://console.anthropic.com
2. Sign up for an account
3. Go to "API Keys" and create a new key
4. In your app files, find the line that says:
   'https://api.anthropic.com/v1/messages'
   And ask Claude to help you add your API key properly

Note: API usage costs a small amount per request (usually less than $0.01 per conversation)
